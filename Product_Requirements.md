# Post-meeting review 21/10/2025

> Goal: automate the gathering of of information from competitor websites

There are 3 main categories to consider for university courses/programmes.

Data points to collect and include in Excel Sheet/Database:
* Primary key = Unique identifier for each course
* UKPRN number
* Award e.g. BA/BEng
* Course Title e.g Computer Science
* Accrediting bodies
* Portfolio changes/notes e.g. Course flag to indicate whether a new course has just been made or not
* Length of study/programme
* Enrolment dates
* Study mode e.g. full-time/part-time
* English Language Requirements are useful to have
* University Course URL is also useful to have
